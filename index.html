YESHUA IMMANUEL

Jewish Scholar, Teacher, Prophet, Priest and Coming King. The first man that defied several laws of Physics including that regarding floatation as He was recorded by several Historians to have walked on water. He is known for performing several feats beyond human abilities best described as "supernatural". Known Root/Founder of the Christian religion. He is believed to infact be God, The Son.

Some know Him as Emmanuel, others call Him Bright Morning Star, to some, He is 'Son of david'. Still, others call Him Yehoshua. His birth was announced as that of the 'King of the Jews' yet the same ones who confirmed His birth mocked Him in death using the same description, 'King of the Jews'. Word on the streets, He is now called 'Bros J'. Many agree He is the Son of God. One can only imagine Him nodding in agreement and saying 'Flesh and blood has not revealed this to you... but my Father which is in heaven'. Prophet; Oh yes, He is a man assigned many titles. Some yet, rather believe Him to simply be a prophet. Collectively, many will agree on this. He is Jesus, The Christ. 'Jesus Christ' if you may. Without arguements, He is Rabboni, Master, Lord of all, yet, Friend. Lowly, Humble, the wisdom of God, the Word of God manifested in flesh, conceived of the Holyspirit, Born of a virgin girl. His birth, just as foretold centuries before. His sonship confirmed by God himself. Eye witnesses recorded to have heard a voice out of the skies 'This is my beloved son in whom I am well pleased' as the Holyspirit descended upon Him out of heaven like a dove. He called himself 'Son of Man'. Man, God: The son.


TIMELINE; (Full Biography) (Eyewitness Biographic Accounts: Mark; Luke; John;

  • 6-4 BC: Born in Bethlehem of Judah, Israel. Present day Palestine
  • AD 10: Teacher of Jewish scholars in the temple at age 12
  • AD 28/29: First miracle He performed, In Cana of Galilee; turned water to wine without lifting a finger
  • AD 28/29: Was baptised by His cousin John and was introduced as the Son of God by God Himself
  • AD 28/29: Initiated His earthly Ministry after a season of Temptation in the Wilderness
  • AD 30-31: Rode triumphantly into Jerusalem on an Ass admidst shouts of 'Hosanna'
  • AD 30: Had 'The last Supper' with His disciples
  • AD 30/33: Accused; Examined and Confirmed guiltless by ruling body. Laid down His life for mankind
  • AD 30/33: Defeated Hell, Death; Took His life up and went to the Father, God.
  • AD 30/33: Revealed himself to His followers after resurrection
  • AD 30/33: Ascended to heaven in full view of His disciples.
  • ~AD 95: In a revelation to an eyewitness of His life, presence after resurrection and ascension, John the Beloved. He confirmed His identity as well as His return.
